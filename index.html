01 About

Craft over clutter.

I’m a multidisciplinary designer and builder with a decade of experience shaping products people genuinely enjoy using. I care about the seams — the loading states, the empty screens, the copy — because that’s where good work earns trust.

I work best at the intersection of design and engineering, translating fuzzy ideas into things you can click. When I’m not at my desk, you’ll find me sketching, running trails, or taking apart something that probably worked fine before I started.
