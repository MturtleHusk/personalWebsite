01 About

People champion progress.

Starting as an engineer working on robotic and mechanical solutions, I transitioned into purely software roles as I explored different industries. Along the way, I witnessed the costly impact of poor planning, lack of communication, decisions made without a clear vision, and undue influence from authority. This experience inspired me to drive meaningful change. Since then, I’ve taken on Project Management roles where I’ve consistently delivered substantial improvements to the projects I’ve led and supported.

I left my most recent position to complete my final semester of graduate studies and earn additional certifications. Now that I’ve attained my MBA and strengthened my skill set, I’m excited to expand my impact. I thrive at the intersection of design and engineering, translating fuzzy ideas into intuitive, clickable solutions.
